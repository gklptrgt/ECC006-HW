Conclusion

Based on the collected survey data, it is evident that online shopping is highly popular among the surveyed group, especially among young adults. The convenience, extensive product variety, and ease of comparing prices and items make online shopping an appealing choice. The preference for well-known shopping websites indicates a strong inclination towards platforms that offer a wide range of products and deliver positive customer experiences. Additionally, it is worth noting that a significant portion of the surveyed group is located in Europe, as indicated by their favorite shopping websites. This highlights the accessibility and reach of online shopping across different regions.

The collected survey data, as well as the statistics from the online report, strongly support the conclusion that online shopping is widely adopted among the surveyed group, especially among young adults. This aligns perfectly with the survey data, where all respondents confirmed engaging in online shopping and belonging to the age range of 18-25.
